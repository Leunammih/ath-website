My approach

A grounded, relational way of working

My work bridges nature connection, embodiment, and relational awareness — held in three movements that shape how we work together.

Cultivate

Intentional focus for deep transformation.

Intentionally focusing generative energy, care, and attention on what you want to manifest — honoring what no longer serves you, and taking grounded action with an open hand.

Reflect

Mirroring & introspection for profound growth.

The catalyst for metamorphosis — reflection is the scaffolding that shapes how we see, and gently reframes what change can look like.

Witness

Truly seeing yourself in how things are.

Together we hold a tender yet resolute mirror — meeting yourself with compassion and equanimity, and finding the distance that opens new perspective.
