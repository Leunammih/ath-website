the woman behind the rituals

Hello, wild thing.

I believe you already carry the answers — they're just buried under other people's expectations, a to-do list from 2019, and that one voice that says “who do you think you are?” (We'll be having words with that voice.)

My coaching is part deep conversation, part gentle ritual: honest questions, small ceremonies, a candle or two, and the occasional homework assignment like “watch the moon rise and do absolutely nothing.” It's serious work that doesn't take itself too seriously.

If you're standing at a crossroads — career, love, identity, or that quiet itch that whispers there's more — come sit with me. The kettle's on.
