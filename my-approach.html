My approach

Relational Life Coaching

A grounded, relational, and integrative way of working — bringing together somatic awareness, mindfulness, and experiential practice to support meaningful growth and self-understanding.

Witness is one of the greatest gifts of our lifetime. When combined with compassion towards ourselves, it helps us to start seeing the patterns of our lives with equanimity.

When we witness ourselves honestly and compassionately, our highest self can begin to guide our lives — helping us recognize our authentic truth and what genuinely energizes us. The work moves through three movements: witness, reflect, cultivate.

Witness

A tender yet resolute mirror

Together, I hold a tender yet resolute mirror. Through reflective practice you meet yourself authentically and gain distance from your experiences — unlocking new perspectives that shift how you think and how you feel.

Reflect

The catalyst for metamorphosis

Reflection is the catalyst for metamorphosis. Within our work together it becomes the scaffolding that shapes how we witness — influencing transformation through the way we frame and make sense of what we see.

Cultivate

Intentionally tending what you want to grow

Cultivation is the act of intentionally focusing generative energy, care, and attention on what you want to manifest in your life — honoring what no longer serves you, establishing boundaries, tending your dreams, and taking grounded action aligned with your authentic self. Pursued with intention, and with an open hand.
